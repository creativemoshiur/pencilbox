About Our Institute

Who We Are

Jamalpur IT Technical Institute (JITTI) is a leading IT training center in the region, dedicated to providing high-quality education since 2010. Our primary mission is to create skilled IT professionals to meet the demands of the global market and contribute to a Digital Bangladesh.

We are committed to empowering our youth with practical skills and the confidence to succeed. We believe in learning by doing, which is why our courses are project-based and taught by experienced industry experts.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, world-class IT education and training, creating skilled professionals who can contribute to the global technology workforce and drive innovation.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in IT education, known for our quality training, innovative teaching methods, and commitment to student success.

Our Values

We value integrity, excellence, and a passion for learning. We are dedicated to supporting our students with continuous guidance and lifetime support.
