About Our Institute

Jamalpur IT Technical Institute (JITTI) is a reliable IT training center for the youth of Jamalpur. Since 2010, we have been working to bring technology education to everyone's doorstep. Our main goal is to eradicate unemployment and create a skilled workforce for building a Digital Bangladesh.

We believe that with the right training and guidance, anyone can change their destiny. Therefore, we offer continuous support to every student in building their career, not just during the course but for a lifetime.
